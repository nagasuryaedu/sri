About Us

Empowering Students for a Brighter Future

Srichakra Vocational Junior College is dedicated to providing high-quality vocational education that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Located in the heart of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Konaseema District, we offer a diverse range of courses designed to equip students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty ensure that every student receives the best possible guidance to excel in their chosen field.
